The Evolving Telecom Scenario

Over the last decades, the number of telecom services and users across the world has grown exponentially. The network infrastructures that support them are built on traditional telecommunications models, where network operators define node-level requirements and vendors create accordingly. This methodology has worked well so far.

Major Threats & Key Issues

  • Spectrum Allotment
  • Alternative Technology - 4G
  • Change in tariff
  • Government policies to be implemented at short notice
  • Fibre capacity of existing network
  • Fibre Penetration for Broadband Quality of Service and Content transport
